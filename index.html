PyroPredictor is a cutting-edge web application designed to predict the probability of forest fires in a specific location. By leveraging advanced machine learning models and analyzing key environmental factors such as weather conditions, vegetation, and historical data, the website provides accurate and actionable risk assessments. PyroPredictor empowers users, including researchers, policymakers, and local communities, to make informed decisions to mitigate wildfire risks and protect natural habitats. The platform is user-friendly, with features like interactive maps, customizable predictions, and detailed insights into the factors driving the results.

PyroPredictor visually represents forest fire probabilities across India by color-coding each district based on its risk level. Built using a custom-trained model, the website combines data-driven predictions with an intuitive, interactive interface. Utilizing powerful tools like Leaflet for mapping, Bootstrap for responsive design, and libraries such as TensorFlow and D3.js for seamless data visualization, PyroPredictor offers an engaging and informative experience. Users can explore risk levels dynamically on a map, view trends, and dive into detailed district-specific information, all designed to make complex data accessible and actionable for diverse audiences.
